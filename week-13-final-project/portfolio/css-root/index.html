Education Session

Artwork for Screen Printing Fundamentals

Wednesday, March 20, 2019 from 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Presented by: Dane Clement, President, Great Dane Graphics

In this Workshop, you'll learn:

  • The difference between raster art and vector art and how to work with them
  • Basic skills in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for creating layouts
  • About fundamental prepress terms and processes so that you can print out your separations properly to make print production run quicker and easier

ISS has been known for over 40 years as THE place to learn how to take your decorated apparel business to the next level and beyond. Decorated apparel industry leaders and experts from around the world provide valuable insight into their own challenges, perspectives, and overall success in engaging sessions both on and off the show floor. Educational sessions are included in your registration costs.
