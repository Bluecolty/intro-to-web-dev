Conflict, Human Awareness, and Choice vs Responsibility

Description

Dive into an explatory and critical paper written to examine the relationship between the book of Genesis from the Bible and Homer's The Odyssey. Through the use of text based evidence, the relationship of these two pieces of literature are explored, and how they dive into current topics. Topics such as human conflict, awareness, and responsibility of the characters. Through examination, links and simialrities between the two works are made.

This paper was written for ENGL 231 - World Literature 1. This class explored famous world literatures, and how they impact the world today.
