Woodshop Portfolio

This section of my portfolio focuses around 2 projects I constructed while in AENG 271 and AENG 130. The first project was a cherry wood jewlery box. The second is a work in progress, and it will be a large tackle box when completed.

Cherry Jewlery Box (AENG 130)

I constructed this jewlery box in Spring of 2021 for AENG 130 (then ITEC 130). The jewlery box was built around plans provided by the professor. The wood is cherry, and the entire box is finished with a poly primer thinned with mineral spirits. The outside was then shined with wax and polished. Inside the lid exists a clay tile with a Millersville logo.

Cherry Tackle Box (AENG 271)

For AENG 271, I designed a wooden tackle box based on the 1940's Abercrombe and Fitch production model. The tackle box has 2 trays, and once complete will be stained in a wipe on poly. There will be a handle on the top and a latch on the front. Currently, the box is still a work in progress so there is only work in progress pictures from start to finish.
