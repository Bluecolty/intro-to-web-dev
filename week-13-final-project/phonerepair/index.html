Phone Repair Portfolio

A little hobby of mine, this unique part of my portfolio focuses on my phone repair hobby. I mostly do this to earn a little extra money on the side of my regular job. While it is unrelated to the course content, I decided to include it to get more practice on web development and to add more content to the website.

Below are a few phones I've restored over the years and some basic information about them.

Repairs

iPhone SE

This iPhone SE needed a screen and battery replacement before it could be sold. It had 64GB of storage and was pink colored. The device was sold for $129.

iPhone 11 Pro

This iPhone 11 Pro was severely bent and needed a new rear housing and a new screen. The device was gray and had 64GB of storage. It sold for $650.

iPhone 8

This iPhone 8 needed a screen, battery, and back housing replacement. The device had 64GB of storage and was red in color. It sold on eBay for $199.
